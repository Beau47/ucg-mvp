# DEVELOPER DOCUMENTATION

This is the Urban Coders Guild learing platform used to teach students from 6-12th grade the foundations of programming
in Python. The platform is a sign-on system divided into instructor and student accounts, and allows for learning in the classroom
and asynchronously.
